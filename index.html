Fan Yang

I am a PhD student in robotics at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor advised by Prof. Dmitry Berenson. I am working on dexterous robot manipulation with a multi-fingered hand. Previously, I was a master's student in robotics (MSR) at Robotics Institute of Carnegie Mellon University, where I was advised by Prof. David Held. I was working on safe Reinforcement Learning and manipualtion with Prof. Held.

Prior to Joining CMU, I was an undergraduate student at Tsinghua University majoring in Engineering Mechanics (Qian class). I was also fortunate to have a chance to be advised by Prof. Richard Voyles, Prof. Oussama Khatib, Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka, and Prof. Huaping Liu.

Fan Yang, Thomas Power, Sergio Aguilera Marinovic, Soshi Iba, Rana Soltani Zarrin, Dmitry Berenson
